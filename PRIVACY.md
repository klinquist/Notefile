# Privacy Policy for Notesync.md

Effective date: April 25, 2026

Notesync.md is designed to keep your notes private. The app does not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal data.

## Data Collection

Notesync.md does not collect analytics, usage tracking, advertising identifiers, or marketing data. The app does not require a Notesync.md account and does not send your notes to any developer-operated server.

## Notes and iCloud Sync

Your notes, folders, note colors, favorites, and related note metadata are stored on your device and synced through your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service.

CloudKit sync uses the app's private iCloud container. The developer does not operate a separate database for your notes and does not have access to your iCloud account.

## Local Storage

Notesync.md keeps a local cache on your devices so the app can load quickly and continue working when network access is unavailable.

On macOS, if you enable Markdown mirroring, Notesync.md writes your notes to the local folder you choose. Files in that folder are under your control and may be accessible to other apps, backup tools, editors, scripts, or services that you allow to access that folder.

## Push Notifications

Notesync.md uses Apple push notifications only to learn when your iCloud notes may have changed, so the app can sync updates between your devices. These notifications are handled through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. The developer does not maintain a push-token database for users.

## Third Parties

Notesync.md does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking SDKs.

Your use of iCloud and CloudKit is governed by Apple's terms and privacy policy.

## Data Deletion

You can delete notes and folders inside the app. Those deletions sync through iCloud to your other devices.

You can also remove the app's iCloud data from your Apple ID using Apple's iCloud storage management tools.
